Start Your Blockchain Transactions

Blockchain transaction is the process of recording data onto the blockchain, which creates an immutable and transparent ledger of transactions. In the context of a durian tracking and tracing application, a blockchain transaction on durian industry can occur among FOUR roles: Farmer, Distributor, Retailer and Consumer.

FARMER

Records information about a harvested durian onto the blockchain.

DISTRIBUTOR

Records information about the durian's journey from a farmer to a retailer.

RETAILER

Records information about receiving the durian.

CONSUMER

Trace the provenance of the durian and provide ratings.

How it works

The purpose of the Durian Track and Trace Blockchain Application (DTTBA) is to create traceability,transparency and accountability for each of the durians and to obtain and record feedback on durians purchased from end consumers.

As a Farmer

Cultivator of durian fruit, responsible for harvesting and providing information about the farm and the durian tree.

The farmer tags each durian with a unique ID during harvest. The farm worker records the information of the durian farm and the durian tree from where the durian is harvested onto the blockchain via DTTBA (DurianTrace Blockchain Application). Distributors:

As a Distributor

Handles the transportation and delivery of durian from the farm to the retailer, responsible for recording information about the distribution process.

The distributor receives the durians from the farm and records the information of the durians sent to the distribution center onto the blockchain via DTTBA.

As a Retailer

Sells durian to the consumers and responsible for recording the received durians from the distributor and providing information about the provenance of the durian to the consumers.

The retailer receives the durians from the distributor and records the received durians onto the blockchain via DTTBA.

As a Consumer

Purchaser of durian fruit who can trace the provenance of the durian and rate the durian for various aspects.

Consumers can access the DurianTrace blockchain application to trace the durian's journey and provenance. Consumers can rate the durian based on different aspects (taste, fragrance, creaminess, etc.). The rating will be recorded onto the blockchain and will be visible to other consumers.
